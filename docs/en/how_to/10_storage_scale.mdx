# Online Volume Expansion

## Use Case

As data volume increases, it is essential to expand storage in a timely manner when the usage approaches the storage quota, to ensure the continuous availability of the business. The online expansion process will not affect the normal provision of services.

## Operation Steps

<Tabs>
<Tab label="Web Console">

1. In the left navigation bar, click on **MySQL-PXC**.

2. Click on ***Namespace Name***.

3. Click on ***Instance Name***.

4. Under the **Details** tab, click the button on the right side of the persistent volume claim.

5. Click **Expand**, or select multiple persistent volume claims and click **Bulk Expand**.

6. Fill in the desired capacity to expand to.

7. Click **Expand**.

   Wait a moment, and you will see the **Storage Capacity** corresponding increase in the persistent volume claim list.

</Tab>
</Tabs>
