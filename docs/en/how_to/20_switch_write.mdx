# Switch Read-Write Mode

## Use Case

This feature supports setting a specific MySQL node to read-write mode. Please evaluate the impact of the switch operation on your business and, if necessary, perform the operation outside of peak business hours.

## Steps to Operate

<Tabs>
<Tab label="Web Console">
1. Click on **MySQL-PXC** in the left navigation bar.

2. Click on ***namespace name***.

3. Click on ***instance name***.

4. Under the **Topology** tab, scroll to find the MySQL node.

5. Click **Set to Read-Write** and confirm.

</Tab>
</Tabs>
